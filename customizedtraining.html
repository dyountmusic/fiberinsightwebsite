Customized Learning

We offer custom training based on client specific needs.

Technology Education

From fiber basics to basic networks to advanced high speed networks, we can customize a program to meet your needs.

Application Test Training

Test requirements and how you test vary between certain network types. We offer training for specific topologies and can contrast multple types and speeds to show how they differ. Specific product training can be offered based on different vendors.

On-site field practical training

Some prefer hands on training in their own testing environment. This can be done with small groups, and may be augmented by classroom discussion to fill any gaps that the network tested doesn’t cover.

Documentation Software Training

Documenting the network is among the most important steps, but making sure your test results reflect the state of the network is critical. Validating OTDR results is one of the most challenging and is emphasized, along with processing other test results.
