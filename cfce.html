CFCE is a five-day comprehensive advanced fiber characterization engineering certification course.


About CFCE

Optical theory

Provides a comprehensive background on fibers, network types and challenges

Understanding impairments

Review loss and loss budgets and how dispersion impacts higher speed systems.

Learn all aspects of testing

All tests are covered independently and as a suite of tests. From bi-directional loss, ORL and OTDR to CD, PMD and Spectral Attenuation testing.

Characterize & document a network

Students will fully characterize a fiber span and perform all tests to qualify a network for high-speed operation.
